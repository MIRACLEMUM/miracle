miracles recipe for the best cinnamon roll

ingredent

  1. milk
  2. flour
  3. eggs
  4. butter

for the dough

  • 1 cup of warm milk
  • 2 cups of teaspoon yeast
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • butter
  • 3 cups of bread butter

    • for the filling

  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • half cup of butter

    • instructions

    1. mix the milk with the yeast,egg and sugar
    2. melt the butter and add to the mixture
    3. add the flour and mix untill combine into a dough
    4. knead the dough for 10 minute
    5. transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.learn it somewhere to rise for 2 hour
    6. after the dough has double in size,roll it out into large rectangle.
    7. cut the roll into 3cm section and place flat into a baking tray
    8. spread the filling onto the dough then roll the dough into a swiss roll
